Questions

Things people ask before they book

Do I need any technical background for the workshop? +
None at all. The workshop is designed for business owners with zero coding experience. You'll use AI-powered tools that do the heavy lifting — your job is to understand your own problem well enough to direct the build.
How is the done-for-you service different from hiring a freelancer? +
Most freelancers build what you ask for. We start by helping you figure out what you should actually be asking for. We bring business judgment, not just technical execution. And you walk away with full ownership — no ongoing dependency on any developer.
What tech stack do you use? Will I be able to maintain it? +
We use modern, standard tools — nothing proprietary or obscure. At handover, we give you a full walkthrough so you understand what's running and how to make basic changes. For ongoing maintenance, you can hire any developer or continue working with us.
I've already built something with AI tools — can coaching help? +
Yes — that's exactly what the 1:1 coaching is for. Whether you've used Lovable, Bolt, Cursor, or any other AI builder, we can help you fix architecture problems, improve the UX, and figure out what to build next. Book a free call and we'll assess where you are.
What if the MVP doesn't work — what happens next? +
That's actually a success. A clear "no" from an MVP at under $5K is infinitely better than a $200K product launch that fails. We help you read the results and decide whether to pivot, pause, or push forward — with data to back the decision.
